## About This Wiki

This wiki documents practical knowledge gained from competing in Botball tournaments, including GCER 2024 (4th place). It covers hardware designs with LEGO blueprints, programming techniques for the Wombat controller, sensor guides, competition strategy, and the Raccoon custom robotics platform.

All content is based on real experience building and programming robots for Botball competitions.
