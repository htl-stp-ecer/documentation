# Dashboard

The dashboard is the first screen you see when the robot boots. It has three large tiles that take you to the main sections.

> **[PICTURE: Dashboard with three colored tiles — green Sensors & Actors, primary Programs, orange Settings]**

## Tiles

| Tile | Color | What it opens |
|---|---|---|
| **Sensors & Actors** | Green | Live sensor readings, motor and servo control |
| **Programs** | Blue/Primary | Run or calibrate your robot programs |
| **Settings** | Orange | Screen, system services, and network settings |

Tap any tile to enter that section. The **Programs** tile is intentionally the largest — it is the one you use most during operation.

---

## Screensaver

After a period of inactivity, the screen switches to an animated robot face. Tap anywhere on the screen to dismiss it and return to the dashboard.

> **[PICTURE: Robot face screensaver with animated blinking eyes]**
